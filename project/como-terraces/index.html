139 Alexandra Avenue, South Yarra

Como Terraces

A signature development by Cbus Property and Sterling Global. Located in the exclusive Como Hill precinct of South Yarra, Como Terraces is destined to become an architectural landmark, with residences defined by generous proportions, abundant light and understated refinement. This boutique collection of extraordinary single-level residences offers exclusive living, in the heart of South Yarra.

Each residence within the Como Terraces is individually designed in a sophisticated palette of neutral tones, with expansive balconies or terraces flowing gracefully from the light-filled interiors.

Residents at Como Terraces benefit from communal facilities and hotel-style services, creating a lifestyle of remarkable ease and comfort. The stylish and sumptuous residents’ lounge area and private dining room provide elegant settings for entertaining guests, while a dedicated concierge makes your living experience the epitome of convenience.

Renowned as Melbourne’s most liveable suburb, South Yarra blends lifestyle and amenity with an enduring legacy of sophistication. Set within this prestigious locale, Como Terraces offers a rare opportunity to embrace all that riverside living has to offer.
