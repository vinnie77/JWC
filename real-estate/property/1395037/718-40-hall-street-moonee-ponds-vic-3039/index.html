Elevated Elegance in Moonee Ponds with Stunning City Views

Set high above the street in a prime Moonee Ponds location, Apartment 718 at 40 Hall Street delivers a smartly designed layout, quality finishes, and sweeping city skyline views, perfect for modern inner-city living or a savvy investment.

The apartment features a generous open-plan living and dining zone that flows seamlessly to a private balcony, ideal for morning coffee or evening relaxation while enjoying elevated outlooks. The streamlined kitchen is well-appointed with modern appliances, ample storage and bench space, making everyday living and entertaining effortless.

A well-proportioned bedroom with built-in robes offers comfort and privacy, while the separate study area provides the flexibility to work from home or create additional storage—an increasingly rare and valuable inclusion. The central bathroom is stylishly finished and complemented by a discreet European laundry, maximising space and functionality.

Residents enjoy access to exclusive building amenities including a rooftop pool and fully equipped gym, enhancing the lifestyle appeal and rental desirability.

Perfectly positioned just moments from the cafés, restaurants and shopping of Puckle Street, with Queens Park nearby and excellent public transport at your doorstep, this apartment offers the best of inner-city convenience with a relaxed neighbourhood feel.

Whether you’re looking to move straight in or add a high-quality property to your portfolio, this is an opportunity not to be missed.
